THE STYLE AND INNOVATIONS OF FROSTED & DECORATIVE FILMS


Frosted film gives you an opportunity to customize your office and home in a way that’s uniquely yours. We have a wide range of designs to choose from, or we can produce a custom design of your choice.

Use Frosted glass window film to add privacy to windows and glass doors without darkening the room. 3 types of frosted glass film available, Frosted, Lite Frost and Deco Tints. All Frosted glass films provide privacy from prying eyes day and night.

Frosted window film is designed to be applied to your existing windows to give it a ‘frosted glass’ appearance and is perfect for added privacy.

The film is an ideal alternative to blinds or curtains which when drawn block the view both ways, whilst also making rooms dark. Privacy window film is designed to allow good light transmission, ensuring that a good level of natural light is allowed to enter the room whilst also maintaining a view. Unlike blinds and curtains, window film is also easy to clean – it can simply be wiped down and clean.


Office frosted film:

Frosted window films are constructed of vinyl film & pressure sensitive adhesive. They’re opaque in colour & emulate sandblasted or etched glass. Frosted film is used for creating privacy in homes & offices day & night by obscuring the view through both sides of the glass.


3M frosted glass film:

Privacy is important & so is natural light. Frosted window films are an excellent option for creating privacy inside a home or building without blocking natural light. Frosted film gives glass a modern, clean & contemporary appearance. They are highly effective as a solid sheet or band & can also be cut to create boarders vision strips & patterns. Frosted films can be printed on, logos artworks & other graphics can be added to give your glass panels a vibrate unique custom finish.


Decorative glass films:

If you have a design challenge, whether it’s adding privacy to a conference room or diffusing light in a lobby, Frosted & decorative window film offers a solution that is trendy, effective and affordable. Offering many different styles and colors, this eye-catching film can accent any interior design glass, surface or window project you have in store and fits your distinct personality and taste.
  • Glass office partitions
  • Glass entry doors & sidelights
  • Internal glass in homes & buildings
  • Adding privacy to glass balcony’s & balustrades
  • Custom digital graphics
  • Vision safety strips to prevent collisions with fixed glass panels in public buildings
